About Us

EDB Foundation is a youth-led nonprofit organization in Kenya, focused on empowering communities through education, mental health support, and gender equality.

Our Vision

A society where everyone, regardless of background or gender, has access to equal opportunities and a better future.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through mental health awareness, education, gender equality, and youth development initiatives. We work collaboratively to foster hope and sustainable change.

What We Do

Mental Health Outreach

Promoting mental wellness and breaking stigma through school visits, forums, and online campaigns.

Educational Empowerment

Supporting access to education for vulnerable youth and offering mentorship programs.

Gender Equality

Advocating for women's and girls' rights, menstrual health, and safety.

Youth Empowerment

Leadership training, life skills, and civic engagement opportunities.
